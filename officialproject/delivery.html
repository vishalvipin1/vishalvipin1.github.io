Delivery and Shipping

We deliver Products to all areas across India

As soon as the Payment process is completed, your Delivery will be Processed. A Receipt will be Generated at the end of the Payment, mentioning all the details about the Mode of Payment used, Delivery Address, Personal details, Delivery Date, etc.

Your Orders will be delivered within 1-4 days, depending on the area.


If you wasn't available at the time of delivery of Product, the Product will be shipped back to the nearest Vidwan Office and can be collected.
Make sure to bring your Receipt undamaged, for Validation Purpose and Security Reasons; and your Product will be handed over to You.
Please wait for an Email and/or SMS from us telling you when its ready to collect.


We keep your order in Vidwan office for 7 Business Days.
All uncollected order will be Cancelled, and any Payments made will be Refunded to the card used for Purchase.


Delivery Option:

  1. Home Delivery: Non-Members- INR 65; Members- Free (Depending on the Location).
  2. Collect From Office- Free.
